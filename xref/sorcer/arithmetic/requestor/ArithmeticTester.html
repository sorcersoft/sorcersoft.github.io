53  /**
54   * Testing parameter passing between tasks within the same service job. Two
55   * numbers are added by the first task, then two numbers are multiplied by the
56   * second one. The results of the first task and the second task are passed on
57   * to the third task that subtracts the result of task two from the result of
58   * task one. The {@link sorcer.core.context.PositionalContext} is used for requestor's
59   * data in this test.
60   * 
61   * @see ArithmeticTester
62   * @author Mike Sobolewski
63   */
